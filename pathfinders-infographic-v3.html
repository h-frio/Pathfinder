IGNITE — Awareness & Capability Enablement
Culture, engagement, digital literacy — unlock participation before the first idea is submitted.
📣 Innovation Campaigns
Quarterly themed campaigns with internal case studies, hackathons, and innovation showcases to build awareness and excitement.
🎓 Digital Literacy Training →
Modular learning paths covering AI, RPA, LLMs, ML, and analytics — tailored by role and business unit.
👥 Innovation Champion Network
Dedicated ambassadors per business unit trained to coach peers, identify opportunities, and assist with submissions.
GATEWAY — Innovation Intake & Governance Hub
One single intake channel replacing fragmented forms, emails, and ad-hoc requests
📋 Smart Intake Form →
≤10 structured fields: problem, proposed solution, business impact, technology type (RPA / AI / ML / LLM / Analytics / Process), and urgency.
📊 Pipeline Visibility
Real-time Power BI dashboard showing all ideas by status, tier, BU, and technology type for full portfolio transparency.
EVALUATE — Innovation Review Board
Monthly IRB triage using a weighted 5-criteria scorecard to approve, defer, or decline each submission.
⚖️ Weighted Scorecard
5 criteria (Strategic Alignment 25%, Business Impact 25%, Technical Feasibility 20%, Effort 15%, Risk 15%) scored by IRB members.
📅 Monthly IRB Cadence
Cross-functional committee (IT, Legal, Finance, Compliance, Business) meets monthly to review and approve queued submissions.
🎚️ Tier Classification
Each approved initiative is assigned to Tier 1, 2, or 3 determining the execution model, team, and budget path.
💬 Feedback Loop
All submitters receive written rationale for IRB decisions. Declined ideas get guidance on how to resubmit in future cycles.
BUILD — Solution Delivery
Tier-differentiated execution with sprint-based delivery and formal stage-gate for large investments.
⚡ Tier 1 Self-Service Track
Guided delivery with CoE coaching. 1–4 week timeline. Low-code tools, Copilot, and automation templates.
🔧 Tier 2 Agile Sprint Track
Internal squad with 2-week sprints. 4–12 week timeline. Full agile ceremonies and continuous stakeholder demos.
🎯 Tier 3 Stage-Gate Track
Formal Stage-Gate with milestone-based funding. 3–12 month programs. External partners allowed, full PMO governance.
🛡️ Quality Gates
Security review, Responsible AI checklist, UAT sign-off, and change management plan required before production launch.
SUSTAIN — Run & Evolve
Post-go-live support, usage monitoring, adoption tracking, and continuous improvement cycles.
🆘 Tiered Support Model
L1 self-service for Tier 1. L2 CoE squad for Tier 2. L3 vendor/specialist for Tier 3 strategic platforms.
📈 Adoption Monitoring
Usage telemetry, monthly adoption reviews, and user satisfaction surveys for every deployed solution.
📋 SLA & Performance
Monthly SLA reviews, KPI tracking vs. approved business case, and automated alerts for performance degradation.
🔄 Continuous Improvement
Quarterly enhancement cycles. Low-utilization solutions are retrained, redesigned, or retired to maintain portfolio health.
IMPACT — Value Realization & Impact
Capture, quantify, and communicate value — plus recognition of contributors.
💰 Value Measurement
Quantified benefit tracking: hours saved, cost reductions, error rate improvements, compliance uplift vs. approved business case.
📊 Quarterly Value Reports
Steering Committee and Leadership receive consolidated portfolio value report with ROI, adoption data, and strategic impact.
🏆 AI Recognition Hub →
Belts, Impact Awards e recognition tracks para cada contribuidor. Clique para explorar todos os níveis, requisitos e premiações.
📖 Success Story Library
Case studies published internally to inspire the next wave of submissions and demonstrate concrete program impact.
Complexity Model

Three Complexity Tiers

Every idea is classified at intake and routed to the appropriate execution model — ensuring speed for quick wins and rigor for strategic bets.

● TIER 1 — QUICK WINS
Low Complexity / Self-Service
Low-hanging fruit solved with guided tools or automation templates
Timeline
1–4 weeks
Team
Business user + CoE coaching
Budget
No additional funding required
Approval
CoE Lead sign-off only
Examples
Email automation, Excel → Power BI report, simple form flows
Power Automate Power Apps Copilot SharePoint
● TIER 2 — CORE INNOVATIONS
Medium Complexity
Developed with specialized internal resources and Agile sprints
Timeline
4–12 weeks
Team
CoE Dev + BA + Process SME
Budget
CoE internal capacity allocation
Approval
IRB monthly review + CoE Lead
Examples
RPA bots, ML models, API integrations, analytics dashboards
UiPath Azure AI Power BI Python/SQL
● TIER 3 — STRATEGIC BETS
High Complexity
Large-scale initiatives with Stage-Gate governance and dedicated funding
Timeline
3–12 months
Team
Full squad + external partners
Budget
Business case + Steering funding
Approval
Steering Committee + IRB + Finance
Examples
Enterprise AI platforms, predictive supply chain, digital twins
LLM/GPT ML Platform SAP Integration Snowflake
Operating Model

Governance & Decision Rights

Three-level governance with clear RACI and escalation paths for every type of decision.

SC

Steering Committee

Quarterly · VP & Director level · Tier 3 approvals, strategic direction, budget allocation

IRB

Innovation Review Board

Monthly · Cross-functional (IT, Legal, Finance, Compliance) · Tier 2 approvals & scoring

CoE

Center of Excellence

Operational · Program management, Tier 1 approvals, intake triage, delivery support

CH

Innovation Champions

Site-level · Advocates, idea coaches, and adoption drivers at each NAPS facility

Prioritization Framework

Scoring Criteria & Prioritization Matrix

Weighted 5-criteria scorecard ensuring consistent, defensible IRB decisions.

25%Strategic Alignment
Directly supports Bayer's digital agenda and BU priorities
25%Business Impact
Quantifiable benefit: time, cost, quality, or risk
20%Technical Feasibility
Available technology, skills, and integration readiness
15%Complexity / Effort
Development effort vs. available CoE capacity
15%Risk & Compliance
Data privacy, regulatory, and security considerations
