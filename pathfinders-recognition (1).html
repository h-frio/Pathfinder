Impact & Recognition

AI Recognition Hub

A simple, transparent way to recognize how NAPS employees grow their AI skills and turn that capability into measurable impact. Built on Bayer's global Impact Awards framework and the Pathfinders learning trail.

We combine belts on the Digital Literacy Trail with Impact Awards so people are rewarded both for learning and for delivered value.

Make recognition visible

Using badges inside and outside Bayer

  • Internal – belt badges appear in the Pathfinders intake & tracking tools, in learning dashboards, and on internal profiles.
  • External – employees can add badges as certifications or achievements on LinkedIn. In future, we can issue verifiable open badges (e.g., Credly) for L3–L4 and major Impact Awards.
  • Program storytelling – the Value Realization Hub highlights selected stories: who advanced their belt, what problem they solved, and which Impact Award level recognized it.
🚀The goal is to make AI growth visible, aspirational, and fair – not to create a new hierarchy. We reward learning, experimentation, and impact, while following Bayer's existing recognition standards.
Track 1 · Learning Milestones
AI Belt Badges — Pathfinders

Each belt is earned by completing the corresponding Digital Literacy Trail level and applying those skills in real work. An Impact Award is recommended at each milestone to give the achievement formal recognition.

White Belt
AI Everyday User
Level 1 · Digital Literacy Trail
Requirements
  • ✓Complete all L1 modules & labs
  • ✓Pass the L1 knowledge check
  • ✓Demonstrate safe AI use in daily role
💌 e-Card — celebrate the first AI learning milestone
Yellow Belt
AI Citizen Developer
Level 2 · Digital Literacy Trail
Requirements
  • ✓Complete all L2 modules & labs
  • ✓Deliver ≥1 low-code/no-code automation for your team
  • ✓Document per governance standards
🥑 Bronze (0.2–1%) — local, repeatable AI-driven improvement
Green Belt
AI Power User
Level 3 · Digital Literacy Trail
Requirements
  • ✓Complete all L3 advanced content
  • ✓Co-lead ≥1 AI solution for multiple teams/sites
  • ✓Share learnings with Pathfinders community
🥐 Silver (2–3%) — cross-functional impact with measurable benefit
Black Belt
AI Product Developer
Level 4 · Digital Literacy Trail
Requirements
  • ✓Complete L4 specialization & certifications
  • ✓Lead a Tier 3 strategic AI solution as product/tech lead
  • ✓Mentor ≥2 Green Belt colleagues
🥏 Gold (5–25%) — enterprise-scale AI with recurring value
🎓Badges acknowledge capability growth and are earned by completing the learning path and applying it responsibly. The Impact Award recommendation is a guideline, not automatic — it still requires a formal nomination.
Track 2 · Impact Outcomes
Bayer Impact Awards — Pathfinders Context

Pathfinders uses the existing Bayer Impact Awards framework with AI-specific examples. We add context and language — the approval flow and salary ranges remain unchanged.

e-Card
Everyday Excellence
Peer recognition · no salary %
🟨 Belt link: White Belt
Recognize everyday AI behaviors — curiosity, peer support, responsible use. Any employee can send an e-Card, no approval needed.
  • →Coaching a teammate on safe prompts or responsible AI use
  • →Sharing a useful Copilot template or prompt library with the team
  • →Organizing a local AI learning circle or Pathfinders show-and-tell
  • →Completing a Digital Literacy Trail level and sharing key takeaways
Bronze
Local Impact
0.2–1% salary
💛 Belt link: Yellow Belt
Local, repeatable AI-driven improvement at team or site level — with verified time, quality, or cost savings.
  • →L2 Citizen Developer automates a manual report, saving hours per week
  • →White Belt uses AI to redesign an SOP, reducing process errors
  • →Power Platform copilot that replaces a repetitive team approval workflow
Silver
Cross-Functional Impact
2–3% salary
💚 Belt link: Green Belt
Cross-functional or multi-site AI solution with clear, measurable benefits across multiple teams or plants.
  • →L3 Power User leads a plant dashboard + copilot that reduces decision cycle time across several sites
  • →Multi-site RPA solution reducing manual data entry errors in product support teams
  • →AI-enabled quality alert system deployed across 3+ sites with measurable defect reduction
Gold
Enterprise Game-Changer
5–25% salary
⬛ Belt link: Black Belt
Enterprise-scale strategic AI solution aligned to NAPS/Bayer priorities, with recurring, quantified impact that materially shifts cost, quality, or safety outcomes.
  • →L4 Product Developer leads a production AI system reducing seed manufacturing cycle time by double digits
  • →Strategic AI product generating multi-million cost savings over several years
  • →Agentic workflow platform adopted as a NAPS standard, materially improving quality or safety KPIs
⚖️Use the same approval flow and salary-percentage ranges defined in the global Impact Awards program. Pathfinders only adds AI-specific examples and shared language. Tag “Pathfinders / AI” in the MyHR write-up for visibility in program reports.
