IGNITE Phase · Digital Literacy Training

Grow the Agentic AI Ecosystem Together

A structured learning journey that takes every NAPS employee from AI consumer to AI builder. Each level unlocks new tools, capabilities, and the ability to deliver increasingly complex solutions — governed by a federated approach ensuring security, compliance, and observability.

L1 · All Employees ~100%
Everyday Users
Interact with AI tools, use predefined solutions, write effective prompts. Foundation for all other levels.
L2 · Citizen Developers ~40%
Citizen Developers
Actively experiment with no-code/low-code tools to increase individual and team productivity.
L3 · Power Users ~15%
Power Users
Build data-driven business solutions to increase organizational productivity with low/pro-code tools.
L4 · Product Developers ~5%
Product Developers
Build production-grade systems and scalable AI solutions with pro-code frameworks and enterprise platforms.
👤

Federated Governance — Applies at Every Level

All AI tools and solutions built within this program operate under Bayer's federated governance model via Microsoft Entra Agent ID — ensuring discoverability across platforms, enforcing security and compliance guardrails, and providing full observability of AI agents and automations. Solutions at L3–L4 require formal CoE review and IRB approval before production deployment.
