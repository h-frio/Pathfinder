IGNITE — Awareness & Capability Building

Culture, engagement, digital literacy — unlock participation before the first idea is submitted.

Innovation Campaigns

Quarterly themed campaigns with internal case studies, hackathons, and innovation showcases to build awareness and excitement.

Digital Literacy Training

Modular learning paths covering AI, RPA, LLMs, ML, and analytics — tailored by role and business unit.

Innovation Champion Network

Dedicated ambassadors per business unit trained to coach peers, identify opportunities, and assist with submissions.

Use Case Library

Searchable repository of implemented solutions across Bayer, with ROI data, tech stack, and lessons learned.

GATEWAY — Unified Innovation Portal

One single intake channel replacing fragmented forms, emails, and ad-hoc requests

Smart Intake Form

≤10 structured fields: problem, proposed solution, business impact, technology type (RPA / AI / ML / LLM / Analytics / Process), and urgency.

Open Intake Form →
Multiple Entry Channels

Digital portal (ServiceNow/SharePoint), QR codes, Champion-assisted submission, themed innovation campaigns.

Deduplication & Clustering

Weekly CoE review identifies duplicate or complementary ideas. AI-assisted semantic matching for large volumes.

Pipeline Visibility

Real-time Power BI dashboard showing all ideas by status, tier, BU, and technology type for full portfolio transparency.

EVALUATE — Innovation Review Board

Structured triage, scoring, and prioritization by a multidisciplinary committee with defined decision rights.

Weighted Scorecard

Five criteria: Strategic Alignment (25%), Business Impact (25%), Technical Feasibility (20%), Complexity/Effort (15%), Risk (15%).

Bi-Weekly IRB Cadence

The Innovation Review Board meets every 2 weeks to triage new submissions, assign tiers, and allocate initial resources.

Complexity Classification

Automatic routing to Tier 1 (Quick Win), Tier 2 (Core), or Tier 3 (Strategic Bet) based on score, effort, and technology type.

Feedback Loop

Every submitter receives a decision notification within 10 business days with rationale, tier assignment, or request for additional detail.

BUILD — Agile Delivery Engine

Execution model differentiated by complexity tier — self-service tools, internal squads, or full Stage-Gate delivery.

Tier 1 Self-Service

Power Automate, Power Apps, and guided templates. Delivered by the business team with CoE coaching. 1–4 week cycle.

Tier 2 Agile Squads

Cross-functional team with developer, BA, and SME. 2-week sprints. UAT gate before go-live. 4–12 week cycle.

Tier 3 Stage-Gate

Formal Discovery → Design → Build → Test → Deploy phases with Steering Committee checkpoints. 3–12 month cycle.

Tech Portfolio

RPA (UiPath/Power Automate), AI/ML (Azure AI), LLM (Copilot/OpenAI), Analytics (Power BI/Snowflake), Process Mining.

SUSTAIN — Run & Evolve Model

Post-go-live operations: support tiers, adoption monitoring, performance KPIs, and continuous improvement cycles.

3-Tier Support Model

L1 (self-service FAQ), L2 (CoE helpdesk), L3 (engineering). SLAs defined per criticality: P1 4h, P2 24h, P3 5 days.

Adoption Monitoring

Weekly usage analytics per solution. Adoption alerts when usage drops >20%. Proactive outreach to re-engage users.

Performance Health Checks

Quarterly technical reviews. Bot/model performance vs. baseline. Drift detection for ML models. Auto-alerts for failures.

Continuous Improvement

Annual enhancement cycles with user feedback integration. Version roadmaps for strategic solutions. Sunset process for low-ROI tools.

IMPACT — Value Realization Hub

Capture, quantify, and communicate value — plus recognition of contributors who drive innovation.

Value Capture Framework

Standardized benefit templates for time, cost, quality, and risk. Quarterly validation with Finance to ensure accuracy.

Recognition Program

Monthly innovation spotlights, quarterly awards, and annual "Innovator of the Year." Points system redeemable for development opportunities.

Executive Dashboard

Quarterly C-suite report: portfolio ROI, FTE savings, cycle time reduction, and innovation culture health score.

Success Story Dissemination

Internal comms playbook: Bayer intranet stories, town hall segments, cross-BU knowledge-sharing sessions.

Governance & Prioritization

Four-level governance structure with clear decision rights, plus a weighted scoring model for defensible prioritization.

Governance Hierarchy
1

Steering Committee

Quarterly. VP+ sponsors. Strategic alignment, portfolio budget, Tier 3 approvals.

2

Innovation Review Board (IRB)

Bi-weekly. Cross-functional directors. Triage, scoring, Tier 2 approvals, KPI review.

3

Innovation CoE

Weekly. Technical leads + PMs. Tier 1 sign-off, development oversight, support, KPIs.

4

Innovation Champions

Ongoing. BU ambassadors. Intake support, adoption, feedback collection, awareness.
